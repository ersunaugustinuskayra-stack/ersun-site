Ersun Augustinus Kayra

Focus Areas

  • Organ donation & trust at the bedside: clarifying death, guarding the dead donor rule, and keeping the gift of life honest.
  • End-of-life and ICU boundaries: what we ask of clinicians, and what we owe families.
  • Border governance & human dignity: practical, accountable models (e.g., limited pilots, auditability, “responsibility at the checkpoint”).
  • Parish-level solidarity / “Sunday Supper”: local, replicable care networks that keep people from falling through bureaucratic gaps.
